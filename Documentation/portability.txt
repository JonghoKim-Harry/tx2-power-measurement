For portability, I used:

   * Standard C Library-compatible Linux System Calls:

        - clock_gettime()    in <time.h>
        - nanosleep()        in <time.h>
        - exit()             in <stdlib.h>


   * POSIX-compatible Linux System Calls:

        - gettimeofday()     in <sys/time.h>
        - access()           in <unistd.h>
        - open()             in <fcntl.h>
        - lseek()            in <unistd.h>
        - read()             in <unistd.h>
        - write()            in <unistd.h>
        - dup2()             in <unistd.h>
        - close()            in <unistd.h>
        - fork()             in <unistd.h>
        - execve()           in <unistd.h>
        - waitpid()          in <sys/wait.h>
